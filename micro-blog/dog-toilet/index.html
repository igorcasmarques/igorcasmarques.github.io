Dog and toilet?

March 18, 2024
Igor Coelho A. S. Marques
Technical writer in Ottawa, Canada

I'm not a dog owner, so I was puzzled by this sign. There are three icons under "Stoop & Scoop": a squatting dog doing its business; an arrow connecting the waste to a bag; and a toilet bowl.

The ordering of icons suggests a sequence of steps. The first two I get: dog poops, person picks it up. But what about the toilet bowl? Turns out the City of Ottawa "encourages flushing pet waste down the toilet, as it will be properly treated at the sewage plant."

Cool. I had no idea.
